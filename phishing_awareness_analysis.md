# Phishing Awareness Analysis
## Description

This project analyzes phishing emails and messages to identify suspicious signs and explain why they are unsafe.

## Sample Phishing Email

Dear Customer,

Your bank account has been temporarily suspended due to suspicious activity.

Please verify your account immediately by clicking the link below:

http://secure-bank-login123.com

Failure to verify your account within 24 hours will result in permanent account closure.

Thank you,
Customer Support Team

## Red Flags

1. The email uses a generic greeting ("Dear Customer") instead of the recipient's name.
2. It creates a sense of urgency by asking the user to act within 24 hours.
3. The website link looks suspicious and is not the official bank website.
4. The email asks the user to verify their account by clicking a link.
5. It threatens the user with account closure to create fear.

## Why is this Email Unsafe?

This email is a phishing attempt because it tries to trick users into clicking a fake website and entering their personal or banking information. Attackers can steal usernames, passwords, and financial data.

## Safety Tips

- Do not click suspicious links.
- Verify the sender's email address.
- Visit the official website directly instead of using email links.
- Never share personal or banking information through email.
- Report suspicious emails to the organization.
